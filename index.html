Project Overview

This final year project focuses on developing an Inventory Management System to streamline the management of hardware components for Zuqo Corporation. Our system will track the inventory in real-time, generate reports, and provide insights into stock levels, helping Zuqo optimize their operations.

Key Objectives:

  • Real-time tracking of hardware stock.
  • Generating automatic stock reports.
  • Improving inventory management efficiency.
  • Ensuring timely restocking and minimizing stockouts.
